This is a simulation of a chaos game. A chaos game is a method of creating a fractal, in this simulation a Sierpinski Triangle, using the three points of the triangle and a starting point randomly selected within the bounds of the triangle. The fractal is created by continusouly drawing points, starting at the initial random point and selecting one of the three points of the triangle at random. A new point is then drawn halfway between the two points, and the process repeats as the Sierpinski Triangle will begin to be revealed.
