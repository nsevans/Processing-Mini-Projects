This flocking simulation is based on Craig Reynolds Boids simulation, which simulates the birds and their flocking behaviour.

A boid follows these three main rules:

  1. Separation: Steer to avoid crowding local flockmates
  2. Alignment: Steer towards the average heading of local flockmates
  3. Cohesion: Steer to move toward the average position of local flockmates

In the example below, you can control how strongly the boids will follow each rule as well as have each boid change colour depending on the concentration of the group it's in.
